Research Prototype — Please Read

ScleraAI is an open research project aimed at building a dataset to train a jaundice detection model. Before you continue:

  • This tool cannot diagnose jaundice or any medical condition
  • Results are experimental and have no clinical validity yet
  • If you suspect jaundice, please see a doctor immediately
  • Webcam images are processed locally — your face is never uploaded
  • Only anonymous score data is logged to help train the model

🌍 Why your contribution matters

AI models need real data from real patients. Without jaundice eye images from actual patients, this model cannot learn to distinguish yellow sclera from healthy eyes.

1

You upload one photo

A clear image of your eye whites. Takes 30 seconds.

2

We manually review it

Your image goes into a pending review queue. We verify it's usable before adding to training data.

3

The model retrains

Once verified, your image improves the model for everyone — especially in regions where doctors are scarce.

4

Earlier detection for others

Jaundice caught early is treatable. Late-stage jaundice can be fatal. Your photo could save lives.
